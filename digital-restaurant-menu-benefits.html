Printed menus can be a headache.

Are printed menus limiting your culinary creativity?

Imagine no longer being restricted by static, printed menus. You now have the liberty to sculpt your menu at will without spending time and energy on expensive printing. With Atlas Digital Menus, you can instantly introduce new and exciting items, rearrange content for better results, and eliminate poor performers from your lineup.

Are outdated printed menus confusing your customers?

There’s nothing like getting excited about a food or drink item only to be told that it’s “out of stock.” With Atlas Digital Menus, you’ll never risk disappointing your guests again. Serve up only the freshest information by updating item availability in real time.

Are printed menus depriving your guests of the information they seek?

Studies show that your guests’ appetite for information is growing. Atlas Digital Menus lets you serve a full course meal of content, including mouth-watering pictures and descriptions, as well as recipe and allergy information that pleases diners with specific dietary needs.

Are you sure your customers are seeing your entire menu lineup?

Atlas Digital Menus conveniently streamlines all of your various menus into one, including scheduled menu rotations. Your staff will love the ease of presenting a single menu and your patrons will love exploring everything you offer all in one place, resulting in greater visibility of your entire lineup.

Is the cost of maintaining your printed menus hurting your bottom line?

Atlas Digital Menus eliminates the need for printing and everything that comes with it. You can now manage all of your menu content from a single interface and make changes on the fly. We’re changing the way people think about menus and doing it at a price everyone can afford.
