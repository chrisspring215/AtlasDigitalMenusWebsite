Printed menus can be a headache.

Welcome to your new menu. Your guests are greeted by an attractive and easy to use interface with your establishment’s logo at center stage. They begin their interactive menu experience by selecting either Drinks or Food.

In one click, guests are already salivating over your specials lineup. On the left, they can instantly jump to any of the categories of items you offer. From Savory Appetizers to Delicious Desserts, you name it, you can create it, up to 12 fully customizable Food categories.

Here, guests can easily preview all of your category items in the order you’ve arranged, including by your choice of subcategory. Want to upsell a certain item? Make it a Featured Item and highlight it among the group.

Bring on the content! Each item opens up into a full, detailed profile including all of the rich content you desire, including pictures, pairings, portion sizes, recipe, ingredient, allergy, and other information.

With the click of a button, the Atlas Digital Menu will remember all of the items your guest liked while browsing as a reminder of what to order their server returns.
