Welcome to Menu Simplicity

The days of juggling multiple menus are over.

With Atlas Digital Menus, all of your food and drink items can be streamlined into one, attractive and easy to maintain format. Your staff will love the ease of working with a single menu and your patrons will appreciate the ability to explore everything you offer all in one place. Our streamlined approach will result in greater visibility of your entire lineup.

Never again suffer through the process of designing and printing a multitude of paper menus. Atlas Digital Menus save time and money by allowing restaurateurs to update every part of their menu through one interface. Our Affiliate Administration Panel is a powerful tool to manage everything you offer at the touch a button. Permissions can even be set to allow staff to request changes to reflect your current inventory.

We will give you a guided walkthrough of the system and show you how it can benefit your business.
