Dynamic Menu Management

Don’t be handcuffed to menus you printed months ago.

Atlas DMM is a game changer, giving restaurateurs the creative freedom to edit their menus at will. Using our powerful administrator interface, you can actively sculpt your menu as you see fit. Introduce new and exciting items without printing new menus, rearrange content for better results, remove poor performing items from your lineup, and keep your menus fresh by showing patrons only what’s in stock.

DMM allows you to automatically rotate between daily, weekly, seasonal, and other menus as you may desire. Create new menus for special occasions, or experiment with alternate menus and pricing. Never be chained to printed menus again. Leave the cost and hassle of redesigning and printing new menus behind.
